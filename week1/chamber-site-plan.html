Montevideo Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The name of this web site will Montevideo Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Create a website that allows people who have little or no knowledge of some recognized places in UMontevideo to enjoy vacations, outings with family, or outings with friends.

Target Audience

Those people who are over 18 years old. Local residents or tourists, who are ambitious, who have the desire to see new places, explore Uruguay and enjoy themselves in the process. That they will have monthly income to pay for the activities they wish to carry out and the means of transport they will use to access the different sites.

Scenarios

  • What is the best beach to surf in Uruguay?
  • What are the best beaches in Uruguay?
  • Where can I enjoy a picnic and observe the animals?
  • Where are the best-known museums in Montevideo?
  • What upcoming events in Montevideo can I attend?
